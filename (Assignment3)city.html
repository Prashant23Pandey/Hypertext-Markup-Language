Lucknow

Bara Imambara

A historical complex with a famous labyrinth. A 1784 monument with a massive central hall and the famous Bhul Bhulaiya labyrinth


Chota Imambara

Also known as the Imambara of Hussainabad, this is a beautiful monument in the old city.


Rumi Darwaza

A grand, Mughal-style gateway that is one of the most iconic structures in the city.


Husainabad Clock Tower

A tall, historic clock tower located near the Chota Imambara.


The Residency

A group of buildings that are significant for their history and are now a museum.


Chattar Manzil

Also called the "Umbrella Palace," it's an Indo-European-Nawabi architectural style building that served as a royal residence.
